PRIVACY POLICY


At GovJobs Online, we are committed to protecting your personal information. When you register on our platform or apply for
a government job, we collect only the necessary information such as your name, email address, and uploaded documents
(like the Z83 form, CV, and ID) to help process your application efficiently. Your data is stored securely and is only accessible
to authorized HR personnel responsible for reviewing applications.

We do not share, sell, or misuse your information in any way. You have full control over your data and can request updates or
account deletion at any time. Our platform uses standard security practices to keep your information safe. For any questions
or concerns about your privacy, you can reach out to us at sagovjobs@outlook.com.
